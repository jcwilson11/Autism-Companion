Step 1: Turn on warm water in the shower. Turn on the shower head.

Step 2: Take off your clothes. Carefully step into the shower.

Step 3: Wet and wash your hair with shampoo. Rinse your hair with water.

Step 4: Wash your body with soap and a washcloth. RInse your body with water.

Step 5: Turn off the water when you finish.

Step 6: Carefully step out of the shower. Dry off with a towel.

Step 7: Put on a new set of clothes.
