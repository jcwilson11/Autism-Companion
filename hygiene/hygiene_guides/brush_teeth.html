Step 1: Turn on water from the faucet and wet your toothbrush.

Step 2: Remove the cap on the toothpaste. Squeeze toothpaste onto your toothbrush, about the size of a pea.

Step 3: Rub the bristles on all sides of your teeth in small circles. Brush your gums and tongue too.

Step 4: Spit in the sink and rinse your toothbrush well.

Step 5: Rinse your mouth with water and spit again.

Step 6: Put the cap back on the toothpaste and put away your toothbrush.
