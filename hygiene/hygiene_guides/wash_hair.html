Step 1: While you are in the bath or shower, wet your hair.

Step 2: Squeeze a small amount of shampoo into your hand.

Step 3: Rub the shampoo through your hair.

Step 4: Rinse your hair under the water until the shampoo is gone.

Step 5: If you use conditioner, squeeze a small amount into your hand. Rub it through your hair.

Step 6: Rinse your hair under the water until the conditionar is gone.
