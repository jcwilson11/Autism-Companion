Autism Companion

A Supportive Learning Platform

Step 1: Carefully take off the pillowcases from the pillows.

Step 2: Remove the blanket and flat sheet from the bed.

Step 3: Carefully take off the fitted sheet from the mattress.

Step 4: Identify the short side of the new fitted sheet.

Step 5: Align one corner of the sheet with a corner of the mattress and tuck it underneath.

Step 6: Secure the other three corners of the fitted sheet in the same manner.

Step 7: Spread the new flat sheet evenly over the bed.

Step 8: Lay the new lightweight blanket on top of the flat sheet.

Step 9: Insert the pillows into the new pillowcases

Step 10: Place the pillows at the head of the bed, laying flat.
