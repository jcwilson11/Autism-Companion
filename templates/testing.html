Wekcome to Autism Companion

Autism Companion is conceived as a comprehensive learning platform offering a range of tutorials, guides, and interactive learning materials to assist individuals with autism. The platform will cover various aspects such as Hygiene, Culinary skills, and Entertainment. Each section is designed to be intuitive, engaging, and supportive, offering visual aids, voice assistance, and interactive elements to cater to the diverse needs of the users.
