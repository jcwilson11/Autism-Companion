How to make a sandwich

In this guide, you will learn how to make a sandwich.


Centered Tables

Checklist

  1. Two slices of bread
  2. Knife
  3. Jar of peanut butter
  4. Jar of jelly
  5. Plate

Instructions

  1. Open the jar of peanut butter
  2. Open the jar of jelly
  3. Use the knife to spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Use the knife to spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  5. Put the two slices of bread together
  6. Enjoy your sandwich!
