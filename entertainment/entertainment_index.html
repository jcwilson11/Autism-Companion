Autism Companion

A Supportive Learning Platform

Search Youtube

Searching on YouTube helps us find videos we like. We can look for songs, shows, or learning videos. This helps us have fun and learn new things too!

Attend A Movie

ttending a movie allows us to enjoy stories on a big screen. We get to see different places and characters. This helps us relax, have fun, and learn about emotions and stories!

Go Bowling

Bowling is a fun activity that can be done wheter it is sunny or rainy outside! We can learn to roll the ball and try to knock down pins. This helps us enjoy time with others, and learn about taking turns and following rules!
