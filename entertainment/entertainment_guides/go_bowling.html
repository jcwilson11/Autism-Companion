Autism Companion

A Supportive Learning Platform

Step 1: Wait in line for the clerk.

Step 2: Tell the clerk your shoe size so he or she can give you bowling shoes to wear. You must wear socks with bowling shoes.

Step 3: Go to the lane the clerk assigns you.

Step 4: Pick a ball from the turnstile or the shelf. Make sure it is not too heavy for you to lift.

Step 5: Bowl your ten frames. The machine at your alley keeps the scores for you and the players on your team.

Step 6: Return your bowling shoes and pay the clerk for the games you played.
