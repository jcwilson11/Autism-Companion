Autism Companion

A Supportive Learning Platform

Step 1: Wait in line to buy a ticket.

Step 2: Tell the cashier the movie and showtime you would like to buy a ticket for.

Step 3: Pay the cashier and take your change and ticket.

Step 4: Give your ticket to the movie worker.

Step 5: Follow the movie worker's directions to the theater where your movie is playing.

Step 6: Find a seat and sit quietly until the movie is over.
