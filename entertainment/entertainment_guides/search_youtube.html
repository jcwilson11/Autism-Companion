Autism Companion

A Supportive Learning Platform

Step 1: Open the YouTube app or go to the YouTube website on your computer.

Step 2: Look for the search bar at the top of the screen. It's where you can type in what you want to find.

Step 3: Gently tap on the search bar or click it with your mouse to select it.

Step 4: Type in what you want to search for. It could be a video title, a topic, or something you're interested in, like "funny cat videos" or "learn to draw."

Step 5: After typing, look at the search results that appear below. These are videos related to what you searched for. Tap on the one that looks interesting to you.

Step 6: Enjoy the video you selected! You can adjust the volume, pause, or play the video using the on-screen buttons. Remember to be safe and respectful while watching videos.
