A Supportive Learning Platform

Make A Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Making a peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a quick way to prepare a tasty meal. We spread peanut butter and jelly on bread.

Make A Sandwich Wrap

Making a sandwich wrap is fun and easy. We place our favorite fillings on a tortilla and roll it up. This helps us learn about choosing ingredients and wrapping them up securely, for a tasty and portable meal!

Make Pasta

Making pasta lets us create a warm and filling meal. We boil pasta and add sauces or toppings we like. This helps us learn about cooking basics like boiling and stirring, and we get to enjoy a delicious dish!
