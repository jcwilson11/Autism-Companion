Step 1: Get the mixing bowl.

Step 2: Add 2 cups of Aunt Jemima pancake mix to the bowl.

Step 3: Pour in 1.5 cups of water.

Step 4: Mix with a whisk or fork until smooth.

Step 5: Place a pan on the stove.

Step 6: Turn the burner to medium heat.

Step 7: When the pan is hot, use oil spray to cover the pan.

Step 8: Scoop batter into the pan to form a pancake.

Step 9: Leave for 90 seconds. Use the spatula to check if the pancake is light brown, then flip and cook for 60 seconds.

Step 10: Place cooked pancake on a plate and repeat steps 8-9 for more pancakes. Don't forget to turn off the burner when finished.
