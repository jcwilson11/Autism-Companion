Step 1: Wash your hands and gather pasta, a pot, water, salt, butter (optional), and your favorite sauce (optional).

Step 2: Boil water in a pot on high heat.

Step 3: Add salt to the boiling water.

Step 4: Carefully add pasta and stir occasionally for about 8-12 minutes until it's soft but not too mushy.

Step 5: Turn off the stove and drain the hot water.

Step 6: Put the cooked pasta back in the pot.

Step 7 (Option 1 - For plain pasta with butter): Add a small amount of butter to the hot pasta and stir until it melts. Your pasta is ready to enjoy!

Step 7 (Option 2 - For pasta with your favorite sauce): Mix in your favorite sauce with the pasta. Your pasta with sauce is ready to enjoy!

Step 8: Remember to clean up by washing the pot and any utensils you used.
