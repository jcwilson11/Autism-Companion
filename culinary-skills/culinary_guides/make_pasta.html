Step 1: Get a pot.

Step 2: Pour 6 cups of water into the pot.

Step 3: Set stove to high and wait for water to boil.

Step 4: Once boiling, add macaroni. Stir with a big spoon.

Step 5: Boil for 7-8 minutes, stirring every minute.

Step 6: After 7-8 minutes, turn off the stove.

Step 7: Carefully drain the water from the pot.

Step 8: Put macaroni back in the pot.

Step 9: Carefully drain the water from the pot.

Step 10: Put macaroni back in the pot.

Step 11: Add 4 tablespoons butter, 1/4 cup milk, and Kraft cheese mix.

Step 12: Stir everything together until well mixed. Then serve the mac & cheese on a plate and enjoy.
