Checklist for Making Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwiches





Step 1: Gather your ingredients and tools.

Step 2: Place two slices of bread on a clean, flat surface like a plate or a cooking board.

Step 3: Open the peanut butter jar. Use the butter knife to scoop some peanut butter from the jar.

Step 4: Carefully spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread using the knife. Make sure to spread it evenly.

Step 5: Open the jelly or jam jar. Use the knife to scoop some jelly or jam.

Step 6: Spread the jelly or jam on the other slice of bread, again making sure to spread it evenly.

Step 7: Gently press the two slices of bread together, so the peanut butter and jelly sides are facing each other.

Step 8: Enjoy your peanut butter and jelly sandwich!
