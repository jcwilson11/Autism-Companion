Step 1: Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water. Dry them with a clean towel.

Step 2: Gather your ingredients. For a sandwich wrap, you'll need a tortilla or a flatbread, your favorite fillings (such as turkey, ham, cheese, lettuce, and tomato), and any condiments or spreads you like (like mayonnaise or mustard).

Step 3: Lay the tortilla or flatbread flat on a clean surface, like a plate or a clean countertop.

Step 4: Add your favorite fillings in the center.

Step 5: Gently fold in the sides of the tortilla to keep the fillings from falling out.

Step 6: Roll up the tortilla from the bottom, creating a wrap shape. Roll it tightly but not too tight, so the ingredients stay inside.

Step 7: Gently press the two slices of bread together, so the peanut butter and jelly sides are facing each other.

Step 8: If you like, you can use the knife to cut the wrap into smaller pieces, like halves or quarters.

Step 8: Your sandwich wrap is ready to eat! Enjoy it as a delicious and convenient meal. After you're done, be sure to clean up.
